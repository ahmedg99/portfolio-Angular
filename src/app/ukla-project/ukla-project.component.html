Ukla mobile application


The main functionality of UKLA, whose idea for this project is starting to take shape in our minds, is the existence of a weekly meal plan; the user can add recipes to their plan either based on the recipes suggested by the application, which will be well-detailed (each recipe accompanied by a detailed video sequence, information on nutritional values, cooking and preparation times, a list of ingredients with the necessary quantities, and finally a descriptive section for each step to follow during the preparation of the recipe), or based on a recipe created by themselves. We understand that eating habits vary from person to person, so we have implemented an advanced recipe search based on various criteria (diets, allergies, cuisines, etc.).
